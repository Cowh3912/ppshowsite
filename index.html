To ensure a structured and responsible PP Art release, 300 million PP Art are currently locked and will vest over time.

Planned Supply Reduction

A 30% reduction in available supply will take place over the next three years to ensure scarcity and long-term sustainability.

2025: PP Art will be periodically sent to the burn wallet and burned. By the end of the year, a total of 100 million PP Art (10% supply reduction) will have been removed.

2026-2027: The process will continue, with another 200 million PP Art permanently burned, further reducing circulating supply.
